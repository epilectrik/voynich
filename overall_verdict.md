## ADDITIONAL FINDING: 40-DAY MOTIF IS GENERIC

Even if the numeric correspondence were real, the 40-day motif is **extremely generic** in medieval European culture:

| 40-Day Tradition | Domain | Source |
|------------------|--------|--------|
| Lent | Religious fasting | Christianity |
| Moses on Sinai | Divine revelation | Judaism/Christianity |
| Jesus in wilderness | Temptation/trial | Christianity |
| Quarantine | Disease isolation | Venice 1448 (from biblical) |
| Mourning period | Death ritual | Orthodox, Islamic, Filipino |
| Widow's inheritance | Legal waiting | 16th c. British law |

The word "quarantine" literally means "40 days" (quaranta giorni).

**Alchemists may have used 40 days because it was culturally authoritative, not because 40 was practically optimal for their procedures.**
