# C579 - CHSH-First Ordering Bias

**Tier:** 2 | **Status:** CLOSED | **Scope:** B

## Statement
In lines containing both QO and CHSH tokens, CHSH appears first 53.9% of the time (609/1130 lines). Binomial test: p=0.010. Modest but significant asymmetry in subfamily ordering.

## Evidence
- Lines with both QO and CHSH: 1,130
- QO first: 521 (46.1%)
- CHSH first: 609 (53.9%)
- Binomial test p=0.010 (significant)
