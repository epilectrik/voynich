# C578 - EN Has 30 Exclusive MIDDLEs

**Tier:** 2 | **Status:** CLOSED | **Scope:** B

## Statement

- Not shared with AX, CC, FL, or FQ
- Examples: an, ct, eck, ect, eek, ek, eod, et, ka, kai, kc, kch, ke, kec, keed...
- AX has 0 exclusive MIDDLEs for comparison
