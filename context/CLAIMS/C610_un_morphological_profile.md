# C610: UN Morphological Profile

**Tier:** 2 (STRUCTURAL INFERENCE)
**Status:** CLOSED
**Source:** Phase UN_COMPOSITIONAL_MECHANICS
**Scope:** B_INTERNAL | UN | MORPHOLOGY

## Statement

UN tokens (7,042 = 30.5% of B) are morphologically normal but distinctively complex. They have 2x the suffix rate (77.3% vs 38.7%), 5.3x the articulator rate (10.1% vs 1.9%), and longer mean word length than classified tokens. 79.4% of UN tokens have PP (pipeline-participating) MIDDLEs, 0% have RI (A-exclusive) MIDDLEs. Elevated morphological complexity is the mechanism of non-classification, not structural deviance.
