# C945: No Folio-Persistent Rare MIDDLEs as Material Markers

**Tier:** 1 | **Status:** CLOSED (FALSIFIED) | **Scope:** B | **Source:** MATERIAL_LOCUS_SEARCH

## Statement

The hypothesis that rare MIDDLEs (<15 folios) persist across a folio's paragraphs as material markers is **rejected**. At the >80% paragraph persistence threshold, zero rare MIDDLEs qualify across all 59 eligible folios. Even at a relaxed >60% threshold, only 3 instances exist, with mean edit distance 1.33 from common MIDDLEs (2/3 are distance-1 operational variants). 81.8% of rare MIDDLE appearances are confined to a single paragraph.

## What This Rules Out

If materials were encoded as specific rare MIDDLEs, those MIDDLEs would need to persist across all operationally independent paragraphs of a folio (since all paragraphs on a folio presumably process the same material). The complete absence of this pattern rules out rare-MIDDLE-as-material-marker at any tested threshold.
