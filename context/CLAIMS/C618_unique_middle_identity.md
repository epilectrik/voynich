## Statement

## Interpretation

Unique MIDDLEs are the extreme morphological tail of B: the longest, most suffixed, most compositionally complex token forms. They are built from PP atoms (95.7% containment) in novel combinations that are too rare (99.7% hapax) to survive ICC cosurvival classification. Their folio-uniqueness is a mathematical consequence of single occurrence: a token appearing once in the manuscript necessarily appears in only one folio.
