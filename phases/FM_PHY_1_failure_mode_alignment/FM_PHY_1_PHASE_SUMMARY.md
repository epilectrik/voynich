## What This Phase Established

### 1. The Hazard Distribution Is Not Arbitrary

The 41/24/24/6/6 pattern is what circulatory thermal systems **naturally produce**:
- Phase problems dominate because phase separation IS the core operation
- Composition matters because purity IS the goal
- Containment matters because vapor cycles continuously
- Rate is minor because equilibrium-based processes aren't rate-limited
- Energy is minor because operators control temperature (it's their tool)

### 2. The Distribution Is Diagnostic

Not all processes show this pattern:
- Batch distillation → higher energy (direct heating)
- Chemical synthesis → higher rate (kinetics-dominated)
- Mechanical assembly → no phase/composition hazards

The Voynich profile **excludes** these alternatives.

### 3. Energy as Control Variable

Both historical and modern sources treat thermal management as what operators DO, not what goes wrong. This explains why ENERGY_OVERSHOOT is only 6%.
