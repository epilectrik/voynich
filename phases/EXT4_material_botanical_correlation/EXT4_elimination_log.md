## Elimination Criteria (Applied Strictly)

A material is **ELIMINATED** if ANY of the following are true:

1. **Primarily Aromatic/Perfumery:** Material's primary historical use was fragrance/perfumery
2. **Culinary or Alimentary:** Material's primary historical use was food/beverage
3. **Easily Replaceable:** Base materials cheap and abundantly available
4. **Economically Scaled:** Historical practice was commercial/production-oriented
5. **Safe to Discard Mid-Process:** Batch failure tolerated without catastrophe
6. **Endpoint-Defined:** Process has defined completion criteria (not indefinite)
7. **Short Duration:** Processing time measured in hours to days (not weeks/months)
