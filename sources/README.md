## Michael Puff von Schrick - Büchlein von den ausgebrannten Wässern

**Full Title:** "Ain gůts nutzlichs Büchlin von den aussgeprenten Wassern"
**Author:** Michael Puff von Schrick (~1401-1473)
**Original:** ~1455 (manuscript)
**This Edition:** 1501 (Ulm, Hannsen Hochspringen)

### Downloads

| Format | Size | URL |
|--------|------|-----|
| PDF | 2.5 MB | [Download](https://archive.org/download/2285019R.nlm.nih.gov/2285019R.nlm.nih.gov.pdf) |
| Full Text | 23.6 KB | [Download](https://archive.org/download/2285019R.nlm.nih.gov/2285019R.nlm.nih.gov_djvu.txt) |
| All Formats | - | [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/2285019R.nlm.nih.gov) |

### Content
- 83 chapters on distilled waters (84 with interpolation)
- Material registry: WHAT to distill
- Therapeutic indications for each water
- NO distillation methods (except Ch.83)
- Chapter 71: Küdreck (cow dung) - only animal chapter

## Hieronymus Brunschwig - Liber de arte distillandi de simplicibus

**Full Title:** "Liber de arte distillandi, de simplicibus. Das Buch der rechten Kunst zu distilieren"
**Author:** Hieronymus Brunschwig (~1450-1512)
**Date:** May 8, 1500
**Publisher:** Johann Grüninger, Strasbourg

### Downloads

| Format | Size | URL |
|--------|------|-----|
| PDF (B/W) | ~60 MB | [Download](https://archive.org/download/KleinesDistilli00Brun/KleinesDistilli00Brun.pdf) |
| Full Text | 1.1 MB | [Download](https://archive.org/download/KleinesDistilli00Brun/KleinesDistilli00Brun_djvu.txt) |
| All Formats | - | [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/KleinesDistilli00Brun) |

### Content
- Part 1: Distillation methods and apparatus (10 methods, 4 fire degrees)
- Part 2: Illustrated herbal (plants and properties)
- Part 3: Disease register (ailment → remedy index)
- First printed book on herbal AND animal distillation
- Combines Puff's material knowledge with method descriptions
- "For novices" - teaching text with woodcut illustrations
