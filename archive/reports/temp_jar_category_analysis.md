## External Category Systems

### Brunschwig's Distillation Methods (10)
- 5 "without fire": filtration, sunshine, ant hill, baker's oven, horse dung
- 5 "with fire": ascending heat degrees

If each method has 3 allowed fire degrees (4th forbidden):
**5 × 3 = 15 combinations** → Close to 16!

### Puff's Material Categories (~8-9 distinct)
- FLOWER: 14 chapters
- HERB: 37 chapters
- ROOT: 5 chapters
- TREE_FLOWER: 4 chapters
- FUNGUS: 2 chapters
- OTHER: 5 chapters (ANIMAL, OIL, etc.)

**8-9 major categories** → Not a match to 16.

### KEY FINDING: Jar labels AVOID M-A prefixes

| Material Class | Jar Usage | Content Usage |
|---------------|-----------|---------------|
| M-A (ch-, sh-) | **0** | 15 |
| M-B (ok-) | 5 | 21 |
| M-D (da-) | 3 | 10 |

**Interpretation:** Jars never use "energy operation" prefixes (M-A). They favor "routine" and "stable" prefixes.

## Hypothesis Evaluation

### H1: Jars = Brunschwig Methods × Degrees
- **Support:** 16 ≈ 15 (5 methods × 3 degrees)
- **Problem:** No morphological pattern grouping by method
- **Verdict:** POSSIBLE but unproven

### H2: Jars = Puff Material Categories
- **Support:** Both are categorization systems
- **Problem:** 16 ≠ 8-9 categories
- **Verdict:** UNLIKELY as direct mapping

### H3: Jars = Processing-Oriented Categories
- **Support:** Jar prefixes favor "routine/stable" over "energy"
- **Support:** Jars form completely separate vocabulary (Jaccard = 0.000)
- **Interpretation:** Jars might encode processing requirements, not material identity
- **Verdict:** CONSISTENT with apparatus-centric model

---

## Best Current Interpretation

Jar labels likely encode **processing category identifiers** rather than material names:
- "This jar contains things that need METHOD X at DEGREE Y"
- NOT "This jar contains PLANT Z"

This aligns with:
1. Complete vocabulary separation from content labels
2. Avoidance of "energy operation" prefixes
3. Apparatus-centric semantic ceiling (C171)

The count of 16 is suggestively close to Brunschwig's 15 method×degree combinations, but we cannot confirm a specific mapping without external evidence.

## What We Would Need to Confirm

1. An external source listing 16 processing categories
2. A pattern where specific jar prefixes cluster with specific content types
3. Historical evidence of 16-category organizational systems in distillation
